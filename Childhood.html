Childhood is Over


(Avatar/Data Self-Portrait) - With p5.js, I created an avatar resembling a watch face that depicts the message: My childhood has passed faster than I would have liked and I wish I could go back to being having a worry-free and fun childhood. The sketch has mouse interaction, microphone input, rotating minute hand, and a group of dots rotating continuously.
