Infinite Warfare


(Shoot Em Up Arcade Game) - Designed and developed an arcade game using Atom Text Editor. The player controls a fighter jet, has to shoot down enemy planes and dodge missiles/lasers. The objective is to earn as many points as possible. If the player's points decreases to 0 points, the player fails the game.
