Never Visiting Paris, France


(Never Seen, Never Will) - This was a project that I created that consist of a miniature version of an object, place, or concept that I have never seen in real life, nor will I ever see. That object is a monument called the Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France. This tower is made entirely of cardboard and has a black and white gooey texture printed on copy paper. A platform was made for the tower as well and can be detached from the tower.
