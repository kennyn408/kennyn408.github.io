Collector


(Digital Board Game) - Developed a board game in PlayingCards.io with implementation of 2-4 player mode and several mechanics, such as dice roll and point system. The objective is to obtain all of your own key pieces within a battlefield and add them to your collection. Earn points in-game and spend them to your liking.
