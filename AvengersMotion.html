Avengers in Motion


(Processing Animation) - Being an avid Marvel fan, I created an animation with Processing, consisting of Captain America’s shield, Iron Man’s helmet, and the Avengers logo. The animation includes a mouse interaction, where red circles on the screen widen or lengthen depending on the cursor's movement.
