Feel free to disregard this page, as it mainly serves as an extra. Hi, my name is Kenny Nguyen. I am currently a 21-year-old student attending San Jose State University. I am a senior with a major of Digital Media Art and minoring in Psychology. I do not know yet what I want to pursue after graduating college but I am willing to accept advice and suggestions from peers and family.


I joined the U.S. Army in August 2020 and I am now a Reservist. My MOS is 25 Quebec. I provide reliable and timely information by installing, repairing, and operating radio, satellite, and multichannel transmissions, security devices, and power generators, and I analyze diagnostics to isolate faults to ensure the right information will always be received at the right time. I also train to set up transmitters, receivers, cable modems, and ultra-high frequency antennas.


I enjoy playing sports, mostly soccer, in my free time. I like to watch soccer, movies, and TV shows. My favorite professional soccer team is FC Barcelona. My favorite superhero is Captain America.
