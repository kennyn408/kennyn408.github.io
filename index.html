Video & Sound Art (PTSD)
Watch on YouTube

The video is about Ramon observing various artworks in the San Jose Museum of Art. He casually enters the museum and examines several art galleries. After he’s done, he leaves the museum. Ramon is the one and only actor in this video. Ramon and Jigme did most of the video editing, such as trimming, speed, etc., because they have the most experience. I did the filming in the museum and provided the background sounds to ideally fit the video clips. There is no music and Ramon does not talk in the video. We used a tripod to make every frame very still and so that it’ll be easier to follow Ramon around the museum.

Zooming Star Link

I created this unique animation using ThreeGUI - WebGLRenderer. It consists of a 9-pointed star zooming in and out forever. The background changes from black to blue each time. I did not really have a concept behind it. I just played around with different snippets and I ended up liking the final outcome.
