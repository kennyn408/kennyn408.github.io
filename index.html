Cheater's Game



(Editing in Premiere) - Primarily using Ultra Key effects in Adobe Premiere Pro, I edited a video to tell a story of 2 competitive soccer players, me and my friend, playing a game of soccer. The game eventually escalates to a game where they both cheat, each to a greater degree after each goal because they were focused on performing better than the other.


Childhood is Over



(Avatar/Data Self-Portrait) - With p5.js, I created an avatar resembling a watch face that depicts the message: My childhood has passed faster than I would have liked and I wish I could go back to being having a worry-free and fun childhood. The sketch has mouse interaction, microphone input, rotating minute hand, and a group of dots rotating continuously.


Never Visiting Paris, France



(Never Seen, Never Will) - This was a project that I created that consist of a miniature version of an object, place, or concept that I have never seen in real life, nor will I ever see. That object is a monument called the Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France. This tower is made entirely of cardboard and has a black and white gooey texture printed on copy paper. A platform was made for the tower as well and can be detached from the tower.


Ring, Ring, Ring



(Let It Ring) - Using a variety of woodworking machines, including bandsaws, sanders, and drill presses, I built a bell with the capability to ring. The bell has a cylindrical shape and can be hung on a hook.


Alpha Kenny's SK8 Park



(Play Project: Skate Park) - I created a miniature skate park to evoke the concept of “Play” using primarily cardboard. Within the skate park, I demonstrated wood joinery and connective systems that can be achieved with a bandsaw. Because of the COVID-19 pandemic, we had no access to a bandsaw this semester but I still demonstrated proficiency in preparing a model for the bandsaw.
